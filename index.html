Our projects

Casa Fox

A woodland residence with distinctive exterior textures resembling rust, alongside interior contrasts of chukum-style finishes and clay floors. Its design prioritizes a seamless connection between social areas and the surrounding garden.

Tigo HQ

A commercial building project in Managua, Nicaragua, featuring interior design for telecommunications company TIGO. Corporate identity colors are integrated into furniture and accessories, emphasizing open spaces for collaboration and visibility among departments.

SUMMIT HOUSE

A contemporary two-story residence designed to integrate social spaces and maximize views, emphasizing natural topography for architectural layout. Open social areas connect seamlessly with gardens and green roofs, offering panoramic vistas of Managua and Lake Managua.
